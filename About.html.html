ABOUT NIGGIZ

Niggiz mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy

Niggiz was founded in 2020 by a group of IT undergraduate students who wanted to prove that people didn't need to compromise to drive electric - that electric vehicles can be better, quicker and more fun to drive than gasoline cars. Today, Niggiz builds not only electric cars vehicles but also infinitely scalable clean energy generation and storage products. Niggiz believes the fasterthe world's stops relying on fossil fuels and moves towards a zero emision future, the better.
